The World Race

This trip changed my life forever. The World Race is an eleven month trip to eleven different countries in which you help the local people with whatever they need assistance with. Some months we helped build schools, other months we taught English. My favorite months were when we would get to walk hut to hut in Africa and help the older generations with their farms.

The countries we traveled to were: Indonesia, Philippians, Thailand, Cambodia, Vietnam, Bosnia, Kosovo, Romania, Zambia, Lesotho, and South Africa!

The most exciting place to me was Ho Chi Mihn City, Vietnam.

Thailand

After returning from my trip across the world, I got married and then my wife and I moved to Bangkok, Thailand.

In Thailand my wife taught English and I worked at a coffee shop. This at times was hard because we didn't speak the language and I had never made coffee before but in the end it was a great experience and I picked up on a few things!

Omaha

After almost a year in Thailand we moved home to Omaha, Ne.

Right now I am doing Covid-19 clean up at a factory in town. It's not the most exciting but it's paying the bills until we can eventually go back to our hearts home, Asia!
